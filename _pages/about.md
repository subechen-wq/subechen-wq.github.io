Tsai Jian-Wei is a Taiwanese flutist based in Taiwan, having spent nearly seven years in Germany pursuing advanced musical studies at the Staatliche Hochschule für Musik Trossingen, one of Germany's most prestigious music conservatories. He graduated with the Konzertexamen — the highest performance diploma in the German music education system, equivalent to a doctoral degree.

His musical training was deeply rooted in the rich European classical tradition, shaped by years of immersive study and performance experience in Germany. Since returning to Taiwan, Jian-Wei has been actively engaged in both performance and teaching, bringing the depth of his German conservatory training to Taiwanese audiences and students.

He performs regularly as a soloist and chamber musician, and is dedicated to music education, nurturing the next generation of flutists with the same passion and discipline that defined his own training abroad.

蔡建緯是一位來自台灣的長笛演奏家。他旅居德國近七年，就讀於德國國立特羅辛根高等音樂院，並以最高演奏文憑（Konzertexamen）畢業，此學位相當於博士層級，是德國音樂教育體系中的最高學術成就。

學成歸國後，蔡建緯活躍於台灣音樂舞台，同時投入演奏與教學工作，將德國音樂學院的深厚訓練帶回台灣，持續推廣古典音樂文化。
